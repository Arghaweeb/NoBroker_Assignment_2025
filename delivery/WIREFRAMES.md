## Design Considerations for Wireframes

### Shopping List Design Goals

1. **Quick Capture**
   - Fast item entry
   - Smart autocomplete
   - One-tap add from recipes

2. **Flexible Organization**
   - Group by recipe or category
   - Sort options (A-Z, by store aisle)
   - Filter checked/unchecked

3. **Practical Shopping Experience**
   - Easy to check off while shopping
   - Share with others
   - Print or export
   - Offline support

4. **Integration**
   - Seamless from recipe â†’ shopping list
   - Smart deduplication of ingredients
   - Quick navigation back to recipes

### Timer Design Goals

1. **Hands-Free Friendly**
   - Large buttons for messy hands
   - Voice control consideration
   - Keyboard shortcuts
   - Auto-advance options

2. **Multi-Timer Management**
   - See all timers at once
   - Visual priority (color coding)
   - Quick adjustments
   - Clear labeling

3. **Unmissable Alerts**
   - Full-screen notifications
   - Multiple alert types (sound, visual, vibrate)
   - Snooze/extend options
   - Persistent until acknowledged

4. **Context Awareness**
   - Timers linked to recipe steps
   - Automatic suggestions based on instructions
   - Custom timers for general kitchen tasks
   - Progress tracking

### Responsive Behavior

**Mobile Considerations:**
- Larger touch targets (min 44x44px)
- Simplified navigation (bottom tabs)
- Full-screen modals
- Swipe gestures (delete, complete)

**Desktop Enhancements:**
- Sidebar for timers (always visible)
- Multi-column shopping list
- Keyboard shortcuts emphasized
- Hover states and tooltips

### Accessibility

1. **Shopping List:**
   - Screen reader labels on all buttons
   - Checkbox state announced
   - Keyboard navigation through items

2. **Timers:**
   - Time announced by screen reader
   - Visual AND audio alerts (don't rely on sound alone)
   - High contrast for timer displays
   - Large, readable fonts
